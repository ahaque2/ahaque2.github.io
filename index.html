Amanul Haque

PhD student, Department of Computer Science
North Carolina State University

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science Department at North Carolina State University. I have been working under the advisement of Dr. Munindar P. Singh since 2019. Before that, I completed my Master of Science in Computer Science in 2019 from North Carolina State University under the advisement of Dr. Collin F. Lynch. I received my Bachelor's in Engineering in Information Science in 2015 from PES University, Bangalore.

My research broadly looks at the ethical and social aspects of artificial intelligence and aims at identifying and mitigating the adverse effects of AI. My dissertation focuses on identifying social and political bias in large pretrained language models and analyzing news media using natural language processing (NLP) techniques. I received the Outstanding TA Award in 2021 for assisting in courses including 'Natural Langauge Processing' and 'Social Computing and Decentralized AI' under Dr. Singh.

My industry experience includes two years at Oracle working on Software Deployment Infrastructure (SDI) for Oracle Public Cloud (OPC) and shorter stints working on machine learning projects for companies like Lenovo, Seagate, and Coupang.
