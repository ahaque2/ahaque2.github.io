Amanul Haque

PhD, Department of Computer Science, North Carolina State University

I am a third year PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at North Carolina State University. I have been working under the advisement of Dr. Munindar P. Singh.

I completed Master's of Science in Computer Science in 2019 from North Carolina State University, under the advisement of Dr. Collin F. Lynch. Before that, I received my Bachelor's in Engineering in Information Science in 2015 from PES University

My research interests include machinne learning, natural language processing (NLP), text mining and social simulations. My dissertation focuses on using NLP techniques to detect news media bias and stance in political news and its impact on its readers. I received the Outstanding TA Award in 2021 for the course 'Social Computing and Decentralized AI'

My industry experience includes two years at Oracle working on Software Deployment Infrastructure (SDI) for Oracle Public Cloud (OPC) and smaller stints of working on machine learning projects at Lenovo and Seagate
