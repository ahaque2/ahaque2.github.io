I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Computer Science Department at North Carolina State University. I have been working under the advisement of Dr. Munindar P. Singh since 2019.

My research broadly looks at the ethical and social aspects of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and aims at identifying and mitigating the adverse effects of AI. My dissertation focuses on identifying social and political bias in large pretrained language models and analyzing political bias in news discourse using Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques. I received the Outstanding TA Award in 2021 and the Outstanding Leadership Award in 2023 from the Department of Computer Science at NC State University.

My industry experience includes two years at Oracle working on Software Deployment Infrastructure (SDI) for Oracle Public Cloud (OPC) and shorter stints working on machine learning projects for Lenovo, Seagate, and Coupang.
